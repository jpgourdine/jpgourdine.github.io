Hi all, I am Dr. Jean-Philippe Gourdine aka Doctor Jay-P, Assistant Professor of Chemistry at Lewis & Clark College.

I teach Structural & Metabolic Biochemistry and Chemistry labs (Intro & Organic). I am originally from Guadeloupe, French Caribbean where I got my PhD in Marine Biochemistry. I did a teaching and research postdoc in Martinique & Grenoble, then moved to Atlanta GA for my second postdoc at Emory University. I worked at Oregon Health & Sciences University as a Senior Research Associate on Bioinformatics projects related to Unknown Diseases and Ontology, Bladder Microbiome and Glycobiology.

My research interests include Glycobiology (Glycan-Protein Interactions), Metabolism of the Bladder Microbiota and Reproducibility of Research.
